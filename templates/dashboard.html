About NeuroPGx Advisor

NeuroPGx Advisor is an AI-assisted Clinical Decision Support System developed for Pharmacogenomics-based personalized treatment recommendation in neurological and psychiatric disorders.

The platform analyzes Disease, Gene, Genotype to predict Phenotype and recommend the most suitable drug, dosage adjustment, alternative medication, clinical reason, and evidence level.

This project is developed using Python, Flask, HTML, CSS, JavaScript and CSV Knowledge Bases.
